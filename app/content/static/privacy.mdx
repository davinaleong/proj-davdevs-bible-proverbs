# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: January 4, 2026**

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Dav/Devs Bible Proverbs is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Bible study application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Settings and Preferences**: We store your reading preferences (text size, theme, translation, date format) locally in your browser's storage to enhance your experience.
- **No Personal Information Required**: Our app does not require you to create an account, provide personal information, or sign up for any services.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Analytics**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app, including pages visited and features used.
- **Technical Information**: Basic technical information such as browser type, device type, and screen resolution may be collected for optimization purposes.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Provide and Maintain Service**: Ensure the app functions properly and remembers your preferences
- **Improve User Experience**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance
- **Technical Support**: Diagnose and fix technical issues
- **App Development**: Plan new features and improvements

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Your preferences are stored locally in your browser using localStorage
- This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
- You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings or our app's reset function

### Security Measures
- We implement appropriate technical safeguards to protect your information
- Our app uses HTTPS encryption for secure data transmission
- We regularly review and update our security practices

## Third-Party Services

### Content Delivery
- We may use content delivery networks (CDNs) to improve app performance
- Bible text content is served from our secure servers
- No personal information is shared with third-party services

### Analytics
- We may use privacy-focused analytics tools that do not track personal information
- All analytics data is anonymized and aggregated

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access Your Data**: View what preferences are stored locally in your browser
- **Delete Your Data**: Clear all stored preferences using our reset function or browser settings
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics by adjusting your browser settings
- **Contact Us**: Reach out with any privacy concerns or questions

## Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't require personal information or account creation, children can safely use our app under parental guidance.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:
- Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
- Displaying a notice within the app
- Posting updates on our website

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Retention

- **Local Preferences**: Stored indefinitely in your browser until cleared by you
- **Analytics Data**: Retained for up to 24 months in anonymized, aggregated form
- **Technical Logs**: Retained for up to 30 days for troubleshooting purposes

## International Users

Our app is designed for global use. If you access our app from outside the jurisdiction where our servers are located, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in that jurisdiction. We take appropriate measures to ensure your privacy rights are protected regardless of location.
