# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: January 4, 2026*

## Introduction

Welcome to Dav/Devs Bible Proverbs. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Local Storage
- **Settings and Preferences**: We store your selected theme, translation preferences, text size, and date format locally in your browser's localStorage.
- **No Personal Data**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, user accounts, or identifiable data.

### External API Usage
- **Bible API**: We use bible-api.com to fetch Bible verses. Please refer to their privacy policy for information about their data handling practices.
- **No Tracking**: We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies.

## Data Storage

- All user preferences are stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers
- No user accounts or registration required
- Your browsing history and reading progress are not tracked

## Third-Party Services

This application uses:
- **bible-api.com**: For fetching Bible content (subject to their terms and privacy policy)
- **Your browser's localStorage**: For saving preferences locally

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- Settings can be reset at any time through the Settings page
- Clear your browser data to remove all stored preferences
- No account deletion needed as we don't store accounts

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our website.
